How to Setup and Use WhatsApp on iPhone

After downloading the iOS version of WhatsApp from the App Store, all you need to do is open the app on your phone and follow the instructions to setup WhatsApp on your iPhone. Find below the exact steps along with relevant screen shots, illustrating the entire process of downloading and setting up WhatsApp on an iPhone.
Note that you will need to have an active phone number to get WhatsApp working on your iPhone.

Download WhatsApp on your iPhone

Follow the steps below to download the iOS version of WhatsApp to your iPhone.

  1. Open the App Store on your iPhone
  2. Once you are on App Store, tap on the Search icon from the bottom menu.

  3. Search for WhatsApp in the App Store by typing "WhatsApp" in the search bar and tap on WhatsApp as it comes up in search results.

  4. Further, download WhatsApp to your iPhone (Refer the image below)

  5. Login to your iTunes Account by entering your Apple ID and Password.
  6. Wait for WhatsApp to be downloaded to your iPhone.
Setup WhatsApp on iPhone

Now that you have downloaded the iOS version of WhatsApp to your iPhone, the next step is to Setup WhatsApp to work on your iPhone.

  1. Open WhatsApp on your iPhone.
  2. On the next two screens, WhatsApp will seek permission to Access your Contacts and provide you with Notifications. Tap on Accept on both screens.

  3. Tap on Agree & Continue, on the WhatsApp Terms and Conditions screen

  4. On the next screen, select your Country, enter your Phone Number and tap on Done from the top right corner of your screen (Refer image below).

    5. Once you provide your phone number, WhatsApp will send a six digit verification code to your phone number by SMS Message.

  5. On the next screen, enter the six digit Verification Code as received from WhatsApp.

    6. Note: Do not close the screen or try to change the phone number, if you did not receive the verification code by SMS. Wsait for five minutes and tap on the Call Me option to receive the verification code via WhatsApp Automated Calling system.

  6. Enter your Name on the next screen and tap on Done from the top right corner of your screen.

    7. Once WhatsApp has verified your phone number, you are all set to use WhatsApp on your iPhone to Chat with and make calls to your Contacts on WhatsApp.
